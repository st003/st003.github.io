Admin Tool #1

This is one of multiple in-house customer administrative tools I wrote to cut down on time consuming data-entry and auditing tasks for a customer. It's a Python Flask-based web-application backed by a MySQL database. Some of its services include: user authentication, customer data storage, automated email notifications, reporting, and notably a data import utility connected to the customer's Learning Management System (LMS) via a RESTful integration. With functionality completed, this app reduced the time to complete a series of monthly tasks from 1-2 business days to under 1 hour.
